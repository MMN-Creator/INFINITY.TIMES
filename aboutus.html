About Us

Our Story

Infinity Times was founded in 2023 with a vision to create timeless fashion that stays relevant. Each piece is designed to inspire, to make our customers feel part of something bigger, something lasting.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a sense of belonging. We want our customers to feel like Infinity Times is more than a brand—it’s their home.

Our Vision

To be the brand that transforms the world’s perception of fashion, creating enduring connections and a community founded on positivity, love, and timeless style.

Core Values

At Infinity Times, we believe in the power of sustainable fashion, body positivity, and spreading love in every design. Above all, we give all credit to Jesus, the source of our inspiration and strength.
